French Onion Dip

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 2 large onions, thinly sliced 2 tablespoons butter 1 cup sour cream 1 cup mayonnaise 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Caramelize the onions: In a skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and cook until caramelized, about 20-25 minutes. Let cool. Prepare the dip: In a bowl, combine sour cream, mayonnaise, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add cooled caramelized onions and mix well. Chill and serve: Refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Garnish with fresh chives or parsley if desired. Serve with chips, crackers, or veggies. Enjoy your French onion dip!

Jalapeno Poppers

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 12 fresh jalapeño peppers 8 oz cream cheese, softened 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese 1/2 tsp garlic powder 1/2 tsp onion powder 12 slices bacon, cut in half Instructions: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Slice jalapeños in half, remove seeds and membranes. Mix softened cream cheese, shredded cheddar, garlic powder, and onion powder. Fill jalapeño halves with cheese mixture. Wrap each with a half slice of bacon, secure with toothpick. Bake for 20-25 minutes until bacon is crispy and filling is bubbly. Serve warm and enjoy your jalapeño poppers!
