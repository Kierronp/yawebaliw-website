Classic Tuna Salad

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 2 cans (5 oz each) tuna, drained 1/3 cup mayonnaise 1 tbsp Dijon mustard 1/4 cup diced celery 1/4 cup diced red onion 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley 1 tbsp lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Mix together drained tuna, mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, diced celery, diced red onion, chopped parsley, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately on sandwiches, wraps, salads, or crackers. Enjoy your classic tuna salad!

Fried Rice and Massaman Curry

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 1 1/2 lbs (700g) chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces 1 can (14 oz / 400ml) coconut milk 2 tbsp Massaman curry paste 2 potatoes, peeled and chunked 1 onion, sliced 1/2 cup unsalted roasted peanuts 2 tbsp palm or brown sugar 2 tbsp fish sauce 2 tbsp tamarind paste Salt, to taste Vegetable oil Instructions: Sauté onion, add chicken, cook until browned. Stir in curry paste, coconut milk, potatoes, peanuts, sugar, fish sauce, tamarind paste. Simmer. Cook for 20-25 minutes until chicken is cooked and potatoes are tender. Adjust salt. Fried Rice: Ingredients: 2 cups cooked jasmine rice 2 tbsp vegetable oil 2 eggs, lightly beaten 1 cup mixed vegetables 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tbsp soy sauce Salt and pepper, to taste Chopped green onions, for garnish Instructions: Sauté garlic, add vegetables, cook until tender. Add rice, stir-fry. Stir in soy sauce, scrambled eggs, salt, and pepper. Garnish with green onions. Serve curry chicken with fried rice. Enjoy!
