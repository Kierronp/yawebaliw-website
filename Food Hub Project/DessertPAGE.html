Banana Bread

Dessert

How to make....

Ingredients: 3 ripe bananas, mashed 1/2 cup melted butter 1/2 cup granulated sugar 1/4 cup brown sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking soda 1/2 teaspoon salt Optional: 1/2 cup chopped nuts or chocolate chips Instructions: Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch loaf pan. Mix mashed bananas, melted butter, sugars, and vanilla. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until just combined. Fold in optional nuts or chocolate chips. Pour batter into the loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Slice and enjoy your homemade banana bread!

Strawberry Cheesecake

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: For the crust: 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs 1/4 cup granulated sugar 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted For the cheesecake filling: 24 oz cream cheese, softened 1 cup granulated sugar 1 teaspoon vanilla extract 3 large eggs 1 cup sour cream For the strawberry topping: 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1 tablespoon lemon juice 1 tablespoon cornstarch 2 tablespoons water Instructions: Preheat oven to 325째F (160째C). Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes and set aside to cool. Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla, then eggs one at a time. Stir in sour cream until smooth. Pour over crust. Bake cheesecake for 45-50 minutes until edges are set and center is slightly jiggly. Cool in the oven with the door closed for 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours. In a saucepan, cook strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice until thickened, about 5-7 minutes. Cool completely. Spread strawberry topping over chilled cheesecake. Serve and enjoy!
