Beef and Mushrooms with Smashed Potatoes

Best food choice for dinner

How to make....

For beef and mushrooms: 1 lb beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced 8 oz mushrooms, sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 onion, diced 2 tbsp olive oil Salt and pepper to taste 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce 1/2 cup beef broth For smashed potatoes: 4 large potatoes 2 tbsp butter Salt and pepper to taste Instructions: Boil potatoes until tender, then drain and let cool slightly. Heat olive oil in a skillet, saut√© garlic and onion until softened. Add mushrooms and cook until browned. Push veggies aside, add beef slices, season, and cook until browned. Mix Worcestershire sauce with beef broth and pour over the beef and mushrooms. Optionally, thicken with cornstarch. Preheat broiler, smash boiled potatoes on a baking sheet, brush with melted butter, season, and broil until golden and crispy. Serve beef and mushrooms over smashed potatoes. Enjoy!

Beef and Broccoli

Best food choice for lunch

How to make....

Ingredients: 1 lb beef sirloin or flank steak, thinly sliced 2 cups broccoli florets 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tbsp ginger, minced 1/4 cup soy sauce 2 tbsp oyster sauce 1 tbsp brown sugar 1 tbsp cornstarch 1/4 cup water or beef broth 2 tbsp vegetable oil Instructions: Cook garlic and ginger in oil until fragrant. Add beef, cook until browned. Add broccoli, stir-fry until tender. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, cornstarch, and water. Pour sauce into skillet, cook until thickened. Serve hot over rice. Enjoy your quick beef and broccoli stir-fry!

Cantonese Spring Rolls With Pork and Shrimp

Best food choice for appetizer

How to make....

Ingredients: 12 spring roll wrappers 1/2 lb ground pork 1/2 lb shrimp, chopped 2 cups shredded cabbage 1 carrot, julienned 1 small onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp oyster sauce 1 tsp sesame oil 1 tsp sugar 1/2 tsp black pepper Vegetable oil for frying Instructions: Cook pork, shrimp, garlic, onion until browned. Add cabbage, carrot; cook until soft. Season with soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, pepper. Fill wrappers, roll tightly. Fry in hot oil until golden brown. Serve hot with dipping sauce. Enjoy!

Halo-Halo

Best choice for dessert

How to make....

Crushed ice Sweetened condensed milk Evaporated milk 1/2 cup cooked sweetened red beans 1/2 cup cooked sweetened white beans 1/2 cup cooked sweetened chickpeas 1/2 cup sweetened jackfruit strips 1/2 cup sweetened plantain slices 1/2 cup sweetened purple yam (ube) jam 1/2 cup sweetened macapuno (coconut sport) strips 1/2 cup sweetened nata de coco (coconut gel cubes) 1/2 cup sweetened kaong (sugar palm fruit) 1/2 cup sweetened tapioca pearls Leche flan (optional) Ube halaya (optional) Instructions: Fill a tall glass with crushed ice. Layer the ingredients in the glass in any order you prefer. Drizzle sweetened condensed milk and evaporated milk generously over the ice and toppings. Top with leche flan or ube halaya if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing flavors of halo-halo!
