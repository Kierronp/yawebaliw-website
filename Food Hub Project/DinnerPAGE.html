Curry Ramen

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 4 packs of ramen noodles (discard seasoning packets) 1 tbsp coconut oil or vegetable oil 1 onion, finely chopped 3 cloves garlic, minced 1 tbsp grated ginger 2 tbsp Thai red curry paste 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk 4 cups vegetable broth or chicken broth 2 tbsp soy sauce 2 tsp brown sugar or maple syrup 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, snap peas) 1 cup sliced mushrooms Optional toppings: chopped green onions, cilantro, lime wedges, sliced red chili, bean sprouts Instructions: Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside. In a large pot, heat coconut oil over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook until softened. Stir in Thai red curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes. Pour in coconut milk and broth. Add soy sauce and brown sugar. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer. Add mixed vegetables and mushrooms. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender. Adjust seasoning if needed. Divide cooked ramen noodles among serving bowls. Ladle coconut curry broth over noodles. Garnish with optional toppings. Serve hot and enjoy your Coconut Curry Ramen!

Asian Chicken Wings

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 2 lbs chicken wings 1/4 cup soy sauce 2 tbsp honey 2 tbsp rice vinegar 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tbsp grated ginger 1 tbsp sesame oil 1 tbsp sriracha sauce (adjust to taste) Green onions, sesame seeds, cilantro leaves for garnish Oil for frying Instructions: Marinate wings in soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, and sriracha for 1 hour. Preheat fryer to 375°F (190°C). Fry wings in batches for 8-10 minutes until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on paper towels and garnish with green onions, sesame seeds, and cilantro. Serve hot and enjoy!
