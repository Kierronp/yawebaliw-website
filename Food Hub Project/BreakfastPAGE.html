Salsa Poach Eggs

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 4 large eggs 1 cup salsa (homemade or store-bought) 1 tablespoon white vinegar Salt and pepper to taste Optional: Chopped fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish Toast or tortillas for serving Instructions: Heat salsa in a saucepan over medium heat until warmed. Poach eggs in simmering water with vinegar for 3-4 minutes. Serve poached eggs over warm salsa. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with herbs if desired. Serve with toast or tortillas. Enjoy your salsa poached eggs!

Quesadilla

Recommended

How to make....

Ingredients: 4 large flour tortillas 2 cups shredded cheese Optional fillings: cooked chicken, black beans, corn, onion, bell pepper, cilantro Salt and pepper to taste Olive oil or cooking spray Instructions: Assemble: Lay out tortillas and fill half of each with cheese and desired toppings. Cook: Fold the tortillas in half to form a half-moon shape. Cook in a skillet over medium heat for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and cheese is melted. Serve: Slice into wedges and serve hot with preferred toppings like salsa or sour cream. Enjoy your quick and tasty quesadillas!
